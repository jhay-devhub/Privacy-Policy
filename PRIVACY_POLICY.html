1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

For Resident Users (during registration):

  • Identity Information: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Contact Information: Complete address (house number, street, barangay, city/municipality, province, region, ZIP code)
  • Demographic Information: Gender
  • Verification Documents: Valid government-issued ID photo and ID type (e.g., Driver's License, National ID, Passport, etc.)
  • Account Credentials: Email and password (password is encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Profile Photo: Optional profile picture you may upload

For Firefighter Users (created by administrators):

  • Identity Information: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Professional Information: Badge number, fire station assignment, rank/position
  • Address: Complete address for records

1.2 Emergency Report Information

When you report an emergency, we collect:

  • Location Data: Precise GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) of the emergency
  • Emergency Details: Description of the fire or emergency situation
  • Photos: Optional photos of the emergency scene
  • Timestamp: Date and time of the report
  • Reporter Information: Your name and user ID

1.3 Location Data

We collect precise location data in the following situations:

  • Emergency Reporting: When you submit an emergency report, we capture your GPS location to direct firefighters
  • Firefighter Tracking: When firefighters respond to emergencies, we track their real-time location to calculate ETA and display routes
  • Address Verification: During registration to verify your residential address
‚ö†Ô∏è Location Tracking: Location tracking is essential for emergency response. Without location access, firefighters cannot find and respond to emergencies effectively.

1.4 Device & Technical Information

  • Device Identifiers: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications
  • Connection Information: IP address, network status
  • App Usage Data: Login timestamps, app interactions
  • Biometric Data: Fingerprint authentication data (stored locally on your device only, never transmitted)
